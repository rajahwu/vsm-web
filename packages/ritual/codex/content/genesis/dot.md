# The Dot

## Definition
The Dot represents a **single actor, location, or data source**.

## Function
Dots identify who or what exists *before* process or decision is applied.

## Operational Use
- Clients
- People
- Systems
- Tools
- Inputs

Mark all Dots before drawing relationships.

## Failure Mode
When Dots are skipped, work becomes anonymous and misattributed.
